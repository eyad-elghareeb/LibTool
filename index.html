Adding an Article (AI Workflow)

  1. Open ARTICLESPEC.md and copy the prompt.
  2. Give the prompt + your medical content to an AI agent (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.).
  3. The AI will return a JSON block.
  4. Open tools/ai-article-maker.html and paste the JSON.
  5. Click "Generate & Download" — save the HTML to articles/.
  6. Copy the manifest line shown and paste it into manifest.js.
  7. Refresh this page — your article appears.

Adding an Article (Manual Workflow)

  1. Open tools/manual-article-maker.html.
  2. Fill in the title, category, description, and sections.
  3. Preview your article, then click "Export HTML".
  4. Save to articles/ and update manifest.js.

Serving Locally

Open index.html directly (double-click) for basic use. For full deep-search and best compatibility, serve with any local HTTP server:

cd medlibrary
python -m http.server 8080
# Then open http://localhost:8080
MedLibrary — Static medical knowledge base. No server required.
